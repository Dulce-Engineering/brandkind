A unique business model

Low-Bono or Pro-Bono

Depending on the project, our members offer their services pro-bono or at heavily discounted rates

Subsidised by Funding

We use corporate partnerships and philanthropic funding to cover our operating costs and subsidise our services

Community Gig-Work

Our community members have day jobs, they offer their services when they can on a project by project basis

1 Sign Up

Register your interest to be part of our branding and marketing community, tell us about your skills, social impact interests and how you want to get involved.

›

2 Explore

A community member will chat to you to determine whether joining the Brandkind community is right for you and ways you could work with us. You will help create your profile.

›

3 Engage

We will contact you once a project lands that would suit you. A Brandkind core member will join a diagnostics meeting with you to assess the client’s needs. You will be involved in creating a project scope and will sign an engagement form outlining outcomes, timings and fees.

›

4 Experience

You will execute the project and dependent on the project plan this might be on your own, in conjunction with other community members, or with involvement and support from the core Brandkind team.

›

5 Transform

Together, we will review deliverables against key goals set up in the project plan and build case studies. You will have the opportunity to share your work in your social media and in future Brandkind training modules.
